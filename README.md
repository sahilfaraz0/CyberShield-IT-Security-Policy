## 📌 Project Overview
This project demonstrates the creation of a comprehensive, strategic IT governance document. Under the fictional entity **CyberShield Solutions**, this repository outlines a complete Cybersecurity and Data Protection Policy developed for a simulated client in the healthcare technology sector, **PakTech Medical Systems**.

The framework establishes the rules, standards, and responsibilities required to protect critical information systems from internal and external threats while ensuring operational continuity.

## 🛡️ Core Policy Domains

### 1. Identity & Access Management (IAM)
*   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Strict enforcement of access rights based on job function to minimize insider threats.
*   **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Mandatory verification layers for all administrative and remote access accounts.

### 2. Asset Management & Data Taxonomy
*   **Classification:** Structured labeling of data (Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted) to dictate handling and storage procedures.
*   **Encryption:** Mandated cryptographic protection for sensitive healthcare data both at rest and in transit.

### 3. Regulatory Compliance & Governance
Aligned internal operations with national and international legal frameworks:
*   **PECA 2016:** Ensuring compliance with Pakistan's national cybercrime laws.
*   **International Standards:** Mapped governance controls to **ISO/IEC 27001**, **GDPR**, and **HIPAA** for comprehensive health data protection.

### 4. Incident Response & Disaster Recovery
*   Structured protocols for identifying, containing, and eradicating threats.
*   Mandated system redundancy and asset tracking to ensure rapid recovery of IT services and minimal downtime during a crisis.

### 5. Misuse & Penalties Framework
*   Defined clear parameters for minor, moderate, and severe policy violations.
*   Established a structured disciplinary process and incident escalation pathway.
